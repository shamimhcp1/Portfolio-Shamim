About Me

My School Life

My School life brings me such memorable memories that I can never forget. Although, I born in Dhaka. I started my pre school at my Home District Netrokona. Then I come to Dhaka at class 3. I admitted to Govt. Sher-e-bangla primery school. Later, studied at Hazi Ali Hossain High School till class 7.

Then I was again come back to Netrokona and finished my SSC from Shahata High School.

From this very long time, I've got some very best friend who are still supporting me through all my difficulties.

My College Life

After completing my SSC I've admitted to Mirpur University College, Dhaka. And started my living with my father while my mother still living in Netrokona.

Few month later my mother moved to Dhaka. And we're started living permanently.

In 2012 I've successfully complete my HSC Examinations.

The Friends from the College life become part of my daily life. And they are such a good friend beyond any good complement.

My Family

I've 1 younger brother & sister from step mother. My Father living with them at Netrokona from 2015. Right after my Grandmother died that year.

I'm living with my mother in Mirpur, Dhaka.

I like to visit Netrokona often though all the busyness of my work.

My Father is a businessman, My Mother is a Housewife, My Step Mother is a Housewife too.

My younger brother Studies in class 7 and younger sister studies just in class 3.
